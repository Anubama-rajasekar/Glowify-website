Our History

Frank Toskan and Frank Angelo started Glowify cosmetics in Toronto in 1985. Their goal was to bring makeup artists and fashion photographers closer together.

Before Glowify, professionals relied on heavy cosmetics, cake makeup, and stage makeup to withstand bright lighting during photo shoots. The founders recognized the need for versatile, high-quality products that could cater to the industryâ€™s unique requirements.

Glowify cosmetics emerged as a pioneer, offering various innovative makeup solutions for professionals and later becoming a global beauty phenomenon.
